What are the early warning signs of cancer?

Here are common early warning signs of cancer to be aware of:

  • Unexplained weight loss
  • Persistent fever or fatigue
  • Changes in skin color or new growths
  • Unusual bleeding or bruising
  • Persistent cough or hoarseness
  • Changes in bowel or bladder habits

Remember: These signs don't necessarily mean cancer, but should be evaluated by a healthcare professional.
